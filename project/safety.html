Child Safety Articles

Safe Sleep Tips

Always place your baby on their back to sleep, use a firm mattress, and keep the crib free of soft bedding or toys.

Choosing the Right Car Seat

Make sure the car seat fits your child’s size and age, install it correctly, and always use the harness straps properly.

Babyproofing Your Home

Secure furniture, cover outlets, use safety gates, and keep hazardous items out of reach.
