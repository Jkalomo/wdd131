Baby Santy Boutique

Website Planning Document

Site Name

Baby Santy Boutique

This name was chosen because it represents the joyful experience of shopping for baby products while conveying a sense of quality and care. "Bliss" suggests happiness and contentment, which aligns with our mission to help parents find everything they need for their little ones.

Optional domain availability: or babysanty.shop

Site Purpose

The Baby Santy Boutique website will serve as a comprehensive online destination for parents and caregivers to:

  • Browse and purchase high-quality baby products
  • Access detailed product information and buying guides
  • Learn about child safety through informative articles
  • Complete secure online transactions

Scenarios (Target Audience Questions)

  1. "What are the safest car seat options for newborns?" - The site will provide detailed safety information and comparison guides.
  2. "Where can I find organic cotton baby clothes in sizes 0-3 months?" - The site will offer advanced filtering options.
